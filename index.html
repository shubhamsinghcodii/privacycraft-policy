Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

PrivacyCraft ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Chrome extension is designed to enhance your privacy while using WhatsApp Web.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

How the Extension Works

This extension operates locally in your browser and only modifies the visual interface of WhatsApp Web by applying blur effects to messages, media, and contact information.

Permissions

The extension requires access to WhatsApp Web only to apply visual privacy features. No data is read, stored externally, or shared.

Data Storage

Any settings (such as blur preferences) are stored locally in your browser and are not transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or trackers.

Security

Your data stays on your device. We do not have access to your chats, messages, or personal information.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
